How it works

Two ways to play.

WebPlayer works on any webpage with a video element or any raw stream URL detected in network traffic.

01
layers
Overlay Mode
Injects a custom gesture layer and controls directly on top of any video — without replacing it.
1 Navigate to any page with a video element.
2 Hover the video to reveal the ▶ Launch WebPlayer button.
3 Click it — the full control panel appears instantly.
02
open_in_new
Standalone Mode
For raw stream URLs, WebPlayer intercepts and offers to launch a dedicated full-window player tab.
1 Open or navigate to any .m3u8 or .mpd stream URL.
2 A notification will appear offering to launch the player.
3 Click Launch Player for the full experience with the 10-band EQ.
